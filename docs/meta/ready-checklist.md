- the study loop is documented
- the syllabus is complete enough to navigate the course
- the core modules for Pass 1 exist
- each module includes:
  - source pointers
  - key ideas
  - at least one exercise
  - checkpoint questions
- confidence policy is documented
- the course can be followed interactively with Codex
